Yaroslav Bayarunas

Bayarunas Yaroslav Igorevich is a Russian singer and Musical theatre actor. Stage actor, concert performer. Producer and director of performances and concerts. Streamer on Twitch.

Alexander Kazmin

Aleksandr Sergeevich Kazmin (born April 23, 1989) is a Russian actor, singer, and streamer. He was nominated for the National Theatre Award The Heart of the Musical Theatre in 2023 for "Best Leading Actor" for his role as Khonon in the play Between Two Worlds.

Daria Burliukalo

Daria Burliukalo graduated from the State College of Pop and Jazz Art, and and completed her studies at RATI-GITIS in the Department of Pop Music. Today, Daria is a theater, film, and musical actress, as well as a singer. At first glance, she may seem elegant and delicate, but she amazes audiences with her powerful voice, strong energy, and radiant stage presence.
